Annual Leave Policy

Entitlement: 20 days per year

Accrual: 1.67 days per month

Carry Forward: Maximum 5 days

Notice Period: 2 weeks in advance

Description: Annual leave is provided to employees for rest and recreation. Employees are encouraged to take their annual leave within the calendar year.

Sick Leave Policy

Entitlement: 10 days per year

Accrual: 0.83 days per month

Carry Forward: Not allowed

Documentation: Medical certificate required for more than 2 consecutive days

Description: Sick leave is provided to employees who are unable to work due to illness or injury. Employees must notify their manager as soon as possible.

Personal Leave Policy

Entitlement: 5 days per year

Accrual: 0.42 days per month

Carry Forward: Not allowed

Notice Period: As soon as practicable

Description: Personal leave is provided to employees for personal matters that require their attention during working hours.

Maternity Leave Policy

Entitlement: 90 days

Eligibility: After 1 year of service

Pay: Full pay for 60 days, half pay for 30 days

Notice Period: 3 months in advance

Description: Maternity leave is provided to female employees for childbirth and childcare. Employees must provide a medical certificate.
